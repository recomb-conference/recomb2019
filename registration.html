Cancellation policy

Full refunds for registration for the RECOMB 2019 conference will be available up to March 24th, 2019. After that point, a refund of 50% of the cost of registration can be requested until May 2nd, 2019. Requests for refunds made on or after May 3rd, 2019 will not be honored.

Social event

The price of the main social event is included in the registration fee. The price for an accompanying person to the main social event is $100. This option will be available to those who answer Yes in registration form in early April on “first come first serve” basis as space permits.
