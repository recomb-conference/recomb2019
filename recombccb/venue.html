Foggy Bottom

Marvin Center is located at the George Washington University campus. The campus is situated in Foggy Bottom, one of the oldest late 18/19th-century neighborhoods in D.C. This historic campus, nestled between the White House, the Kennedy Center and Lincoln Memorial.

Getting to Foggy Bottom

Foggy Bottom is conveniently located in the heart of Washington, D.C., close to three major airports, including Ronald Reagan Washington National, Washington Dulles International and Baltimore Washington International, as well as to Union Station where you can take the bus or train. How to reach Foggy Bottom from airports can be found here.

Metro Stations
  • Foggy Bottom (Orange, Silver, Blue Lines, 0.3 miles)
  • Farragut North (Red Line, 0.6 miles)
Reaching the Venue

From Foggy Bottom Metro, take a right onto 23rd Street NW followed by a left (east) turn into H Street NW. Continue for two blocks and you will find the venue on your left at the intersection with 21st Street NW. From Farragut North Metro, stay on K Street NW and walk 4 blocks west until you reach 21st Street NW. Take a left on 21st Street NW and continue until H Street NW. The venue will be on your right side.

Parking

If you’re driving to campus, you can enter the Marvin Center’s address of 800 21st Street NW into your GPS as a reference point or head right to one of our visitor parking garages. Be sure to review the parking garage rates and hours before you arrive.

WiFi

Two wireless networks are available across the GW campus: GWconnect (available to everyone upon free registration), and eduroam (available to attendees from participating institutions). Please visit the webpage for details.
